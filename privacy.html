Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Corvee Companion ("the app") is a free desktop application published by Corvee Paper, an independent studio based in France. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we process

  • The app retrieves publicly available League of Legends account and match data from the official Riot Games API, at your request — for example when you look up a Riot ID or open your match history.
  • This data is cached locally on your own device to improve performance and reduce repeated requests.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not display advertising and do not use third-party trackers.
  • We do not ask for, or store, your Riot account password — the app only uses public data and Riot's official APIs.

Riot Games API

The app uses the Riot Games API in accordance with Riot's Developer Agreement and policies. All game data displayed belongs to Riot Games.

Contact

For any question about this policy or your data, contact us at contact@corveepaper.fr.

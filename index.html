What it does

Everything to review your ranked grind

Powered by the official Riot Games API.

Detailed match history

Every game with a full post-game scoreboard — KDA, damage, gold, builds and runes for all ten players.

Champion & role stats

See what's actually working: win rate and KDA broken down by champion and by role, at a glance.

Season analysis

Track your form across the season — win-rate trends, KDA over time, and how you compare to your rank.

Live game summary

A clean at-a-glance view of your own in-progress game, using Riot's official Live Client Data API on your machine.
